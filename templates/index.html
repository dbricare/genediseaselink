Market Volatility

Market volatility is calculated by the Chicago Board Options Exchange (CBOE) and is expressed as the portion of the annualized stock movement expected (with 68% probability) to occur over the next 30 days.

For example, a VIX volatility value of 15 represents a maximum 15% expected annualized change with 68% probability. Thus the indicated stock or index is expected to change +/- 15%/sqrt(12) = 4.33% over the next 30 days. Reference: Wikipedia.
